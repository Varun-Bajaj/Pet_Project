FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pet sharing platform.

How do I find a reliable pet sitter?
All our pet sitters are thoroughly vetted with background checks, references, and reviews from other pet owners. You can browse profiles, read reviews, and even meet potential sitters before making your decision.
What if my pet has special needs?
Many of our sitters specialize in caring for pets with special needs, including medication administration, mobility assistance, and dietary restrictions. You can filter sitters based on their experience with specific requirements.
How much does pet sitting cost?
Pricing varies based on location, services needed, and duration. Our platform shows transparent pricing upfront, and you can compare rates from different sitters in your area. Most services range from $25-75 per day.
What happens in case of an emergency?
We provide 24/7 emergency support and all our sitters are trained in basic pet first aid. We also maintain partnerships with local veterinary clinics and have emergency protocols in place for urgent situations.
Can I meet the sitter before booking?
Absolutely! We encourage meet-and-greets between pets, owners, and sitters. This helps ensure compatibility and gives you peace of mind before leaving your pet in someone else's care.
